About Me

Without the Hope is Tal Aviram's debut solo album.

It's made out of simple songs & great singers wrapped by beautiful production.
This album was made with the purity of making great music for free with hope the people will like it enough to share it.

You can download & listen to the whole album for free on the website.

Tal Aviram -
Producer, Programmer, Musician.
Working mostly as Programmer & Pro Tools Operator with Israel's leading artists (including - Hayehudim, Mosaic Project, Tal Segev, Yaya - Hadag Nahas, Shiri Maymon and many more)

Without the Hope reveals his unique approach to sound arrangements, programming and mixing.
